# THE CONSULTING FACTORY: CORE TEMPLATES
**Stage 1: Building the Engine**

*As per the new strategy, do not reinvent the wheel for every company. Copy-paste these templates, fill them with deep research, and publish. This is how you scale from 1 project to 15 without burning out.*

---

## 1. THE MASTER RESEARCH TEMPLATE (25-40 Pages)
*Save this as `[Company]_Research_Template.docx`*

**1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY**
*   The Problem Statement (What is the core existential question for this company?)
*   The Core Thesis (Your 1-paragraph verdict on their strategy).

**2. COMPANY OVERVIEW**
*   Founding Story & Leadership.
*   The Business Model (How do they actually make money?).

**3. UNIT ECONOMICS WATERFALL**
*   Average Order Value (AOV) / Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) / Lifetime Value (LTV).
*   *Template Question:* What is the gross margin on a single transaction?

**4. STRATEGIC FRAMEWORKS**
*   **VRIO Analysis:** What is their True Moat? (Is it Valuable, Rare, Inimitable, Organized?)
*   **Porter’s Five Forces:** Threat of New Entrants, Bargaining Power of Suppliers/Buyers, Threat of Substitutes, Rivalry.
*   **PESTLE Analysis:** Focus heavily on Regulatory (L) and Economic (E) risks.

**5. THE COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE**
*   Direct vs. Indirect Competitors.
*   Market Share breakdown.

**6. STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS (The "So What?")**
*   3 actionable steps the CEO should take tomorrow.
*   Must be backed by data, not generic advice.

## 2. THE MCKINSEY PPT TEMPLATE (15 Slides)
*Use a minimalist Canva template. White background, dark grey text, one primary accent color.*

*   **Slide 3: The Market Landscape** (TAM/SAM/SOM).

## 3. LINKEDIN POST TEMPLATES (300-700 Words)

### Template A: The Contrarian Teardown
**Hook:** Everyone thinks [Company] wins because of [Obvious Reason]. They are completely wrong.
**Body:** 
*   Explain the mainstream narrative (e.g., "People think Zepto wins because of 10-minute delivery").
*   Introduce the hidden engine (e.g., "The real reason they win is Algorithmic Density and ad revenue").
*   Back it up with 2 data points from your research.
**CTA:** I spent 20 hours mapping their exact path to profitability. Link to the full 30-page MBB report in the comments.

### Template B: The Unit Economics Expose
**Hook:** How does a company lose money on a ₹500 order, but make millions overall? Let’s look at [Company].
**Body:**
*   Break down the unit economics waterfall (Cost of goods + Delivery + Marketing).
*   Show the exact point where they hit profitability (e.g., "They only make money if you buy a ₹1,200 electronics item").
*   Explain the strategic pivot they must make.
**CTA:** Want the full financial breakdown? Check my Topmate for the complete case study.
