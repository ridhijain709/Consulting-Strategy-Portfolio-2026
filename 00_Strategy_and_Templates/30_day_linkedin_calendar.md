### 💡 DAILY ENGAGEMENT ROUTINE (The "Hidden" 30 Days)
Writing the post is only 50% of the work. Spend 30 minutes every day doing this:
1.  **Comment on 5 Target Accounts:** Find posts from MBB Partners, Startup Founders, and Top Voices in Strategy/Retail. Leave highly analytical comments (e.g., apply the VRIO framework to their post). Do not leave "Great post!" comments.
2.  **Reply to Every Comment:** If someone comments on your post, reply with a follow-up question to spark a secondary thread. This forces the LinkedIn algorithm to push your post to their network.
3.  **Outbound DMs:** Send 2 targeted connection requests a day to recruiters or founders. Note: "Hi [Name], I just published a 30-page strategic teardown on Nykaa's margin defense against Reliance Tira. Would love your feedback on my unit economics assumptions."
