## PART 1: MASTER RESEARCH DOCUMENT

### 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Zomato has transitioned from a restaurant discovery app to an incredibly profitable logistics and B2B ecosystem. By achieving consistent PAT profitability, Zomato silenced critics of the gig-economy model. Its acquisition of Blinkit (Quick Commerce) and Hyperpure (B2B Restaurant Supply) transformed it into a diversified conglomerate. Zomato's strategic brilliance lies in unbundling its apps (Zomato for food, Blinkit for groceries) while centralizing its data and capital allocation. 

### 2. BUSINESS & ECONOMIC MODEL
*   **Food Delivery:** Highly profitable. AOV has stabilized, and platform fees (₹4-₹6 per order) drop straight to the bottom line.
*   **Blinkit (Quick Commerce):** The growth engine. 45% market share.
*   **Hyperpure (B2B):** Supplying fresh ingredients to restaurants. Low margin, but massive GMV and locks restaurants into the Zomato ecosystem.
*   **Going Out (Events/Dining):** The acquisition of Paytm's ticketing business signals a move to dominate urban out-of-home entertainment.

### 3. VRIO ANALYSIS & MOAT
*   **Hyperpure (B2B Supply):** **Valuable, Rare, Inimitable.** Competitors cannot easily replicate a nationwide B2B cold-chain supply for restaurants.
*   **Top-of-Funnel Traffic:** Massive organic MAUs reduce CAC for new ventures (like Blinkit or Events) to near zero.

### 4. STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS
*   **Dominate the 'Going Out' Category:** Integrate movie/event ticketing to capture the entire Friday night wallet (Dining + Entertainment).
*   **Scale Blinkit Ads:** Match Amazon's retail media margins.
