# 01 · ZEPTO
## Quick Commerce · Unit Economics · Path to Profitability

---

<!-- _class: split -->

![Zepto Revenue Growth](assets/zepto_3d.png)

# Revenue Trajectory

**$1.4B valuation → $5B+ in 24 months.**

- FY24 Revenue: ₹4,454 Cr (+250% YoY)
- FY25 Revenue: ₹7,500+ Cr (est.)
- Contribution margin turned positive Q3 FY25
- AOV expanded from ₹350 → ₹500+ via category expansion

**Core Thesis:** Speed is the moat. 10-minute delivery creates habitual usage that competitors cannot match without identical dark store density.

---

# Zepto · Strategic Framework

| Framework | Assessment |
|-----------|------------|
| **Porter's 5 Forces** | High rivalry (Blinkit, Instamart). Low supplier power. High buyer power. |
| **VRIO** | Dark store density = Valuable + Rare. Speed promise = Temporary advantage. |
| **Unit Economics** | Delivery cost ₹35-40/order. Break-even AOV: ₹450. Current AOV: ₹500+. |
| **Revenue Mix** | Platform fees (40%), Advertising (35%), Delivery fees (15%), Zepto Pass (10%) |

## Strategic Recommendations
- **Scale Zepto Ads** to ₹2,000 Cr run-rate (highest-margin revenue stream)
- **Expand Zepto Cafe** into every dark store to capture food delivery TAM
- **IPO in 2026-27** while market sentiment for quick commerce is bullish

# 02 · NYKAA
## Beauty & Personal Care · DTC Moat · Omnichannel Execution

---

<!-- _class: split -->

![Nykaa Market Share](assets/nykaa_3d.png)

# The DTC Moat Under Siege

**India's #1 beauty platform faces an existential question:** Can it defend premium positioning against Reliance and Tata?

- 30M+ active customers
- 6,000+ brands, 4M+ SKUs
- Gross margins: 42-45% (luxury beauty)
- Private label (Nykaa Cosmetics) drives highest margin

**The Risk:** Trent's Zudio and Reliance's Tira are attacking from the value and luxury ends simultaneously.

---

# Nykaa · Competitive Position

| Competitor | Threat Level | Strategy |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| **Tira (Reliance)** | 🔴 Critical | Aggressive luxury brand acquisition, unlimited capital |
| **Purplle** | 🟡 Medium | Value segment, strong in Tier 2-3 cities |
| **Amazon Beauty** | 🟡 Medium | Convenience play, weak curation |
| **Sugar Cosmetics** | 🟢 Low | DTC-only, limited distribution |

## Strategic Recommendations
- **Double down on content commerce** — tutorials, reviews, and community create switching costs that Reliance cannot replicate with capital alone
- **Accelerate physical retail** to 300+ stores by FY27
- **Defend private label margins** as the primary profit engine

# 03 · BLINKIT
## Quick Commerce · Zero-CAC Advantage · AOV Expansion

---

<!-- _class: split -->

![Blinkit CAC Comparison](assets/blinkit_3d.png)

# The Zero-CAC Ecosystem Moat

**Why Blinkit wins structurally:**

While Zepto burns ₹150-300 per customer on Meta/Google ads, Blinkit acquires users for **₹0** through the Zomato app tab.

- 45% quick commerce market share
- Cross-sell from Zomato's 80M+ monthly active users
- Capital saved on CAC is invested in dark store density
- Result: Faster path to EBITDA parity

> "Blinkit doesn't buy users. It borrows them from Zomato."

---

# Blinkit · The AOV Expansion Thesis

Grocery at ₹400 AOV is loss-making. The solution is **category expansion**.

| Category | AOV | Contribution Margin | Strategic Role |
|----------|-----|---------------------|----------------|
| Groceries | ₹400 | Negative | Traffic driver |
| Beauty & Personal Care | ₹1,200 | Positive | Margin builder |
| Electronics | ₹35,000 | Highly Profitable | Profit engine |
| Printouts & Utilities | ₹100 | Positive | Frequency driver |

## The Insight
Delivering a PlayStation 5 costs the same ₹40 delivery fee as delivering milk — but the absolute margin is **100x higher**. This is the "Amazon-in-10-minutes" model.

# 04 · SWIGGY
## Food Delivery · Super-App Bundling · Instamart Breakout

---

<!-- _class: split -->

![Swiggy Growth Layers](assets/swiggy_3d.png)

# The Two-Front War

**Swiggy fights Zomato on food and Zepto/Blinkit on quick commerce simultaneously.**

The only path to victory: **Swiggy One subscription** — lock users into the ecosystem.

- Swiggy One members order **3x more** frequently
- Bundled value: Food + Instamart + Dineout discounts
- Creates switching costs that individual apps cannot match

**Post-IPO Reality:** Public markets demand a clear path to consolidated profitability. Swiggy must prove Instamart won't bleed forever.

---

# Swiggy · Strategic Roadmap

| Strategic Pillar | Action | Timeline |
|-----------------|--------|----------|
| **Super-App Bundling** | Tighter integration of Dineout + Instamart + Food | H2 2026 |
| **Fleet Optimization** | Share delivery fleet across food and groceries | Ongoing |
| **Profitability Push** | Reduce Instamart burn rate by 40% | FY27 |
| **Swiggy One Penetration** | Target 50% of orders from subscribers | FY27 |

## The Key Question for Interviewers
*"How would you advise Swiggy to allocate capital between defending food delivery market share versus growing Instamart, given that each dollar invested in Instamart has a negative near-term ROI?"*

# 05 · ZOMATO
## Consumer Tech Conglomerate · Ecosystem Strategy · Beyond Food

---

<!-- _class: split -->

![Zomato Ecosystem](assets/zomato_3d.png)

# Beyond Food: The Conglomerate Play

**Zomato is no longer a food delivery company.** It is a diversified consumer tech conglomerate.

- **Zomato Food:** Cash cow. Generates profits to fund moonshots.
- **Blinkit:** Growth engine. Fastest-growing vertical.
- **Hyperpure:** B2B supply chain moat. Locks in restaurant partners.
- **District (Going Out):** Events, dining, ticketing. Blue ocean with 60%+ margins.

**Deepinder's thesis:** Run each vertical as an independent company, sharing a unified data backend.

---

# Zomato · Conglomerate Valuation

| Vertical | Revenue Contribution | Growth Rate | Margin Profile |
|----------|---------------------|-------------|----------------|
| Food Delivery | 55% | 15% YoY | Profitable |
| Blinkit | 30% | 120% YoY | Approaching break-even |
| Hyperpure | 10% | 40% YoY | Low but strategic |
| District | 5% | 200%+ YoY | Very high |

## Strategic Recommendations
- **Scale District** to rival BookMyShow. The "going out" market is a blue ocean for cross-selling Zomato's dining users.
- **Hyperpure moat:** Become the default B2B supplier for every urban restaurant in India.
- **Decentralized structure:** Each vertical should have independent P&L accountability.

# 06 · RAZORPAY
## B2B Fintech · Financial Operating System · SaaS Pivot

---

<!-- _class: split -->

![Razorpay Revenue Funnel](assets/razorpay_3d.png)

# The Commoditization Escape

**Payment gateways are a commodity.** UPI's zero MDR means payments alone cannot sustain a high-value business.

**Razorpay's genius:** Use payments as a **customer acquisition tool**, then cross-sell high-margin B2B SaaS.

- **Gateway:** Acquires merchants (1-3% fee, low margin)
- **RazorpayX:** Business banking suite (subscriptions, high margin)
- **Payroll:** Automated salary + compliance SaaS (very high margin)
- **Capital:** AI-underwritten working capital loans (highest margin)

> Once a merchant uses Razorpay for payments, RazorpayX for payouts, and Payroll for salaries — they never leave.

---

# Razorpay · VRIO & Moat Analysis

| Resource | Valuable | Rare | Inimitable | Organized | Verdict |
|----------|:--------:|:----:|:----------:|:---------:|---------|
| Payment Gateway | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ | Competitive Parity |
| API Developer Experience | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ✅ | Temporary Advantage |
| **Unified Data Graph** | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | **Sustained Moat** |
| **Ecosystem Lock-in** | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | ✅ | **Sustained Moat** |

## The Data Advantage
Because Razorpay sees a merchant's **incoming revenue** (Gateway) and **outgoing expenses** (RazorpayX), they possess the ultimate data graph to underwrite working capital loans with near-zero default risk.

**IPO Positioning:** Not a "Payments Company" (low multiple) but a "B2B Financial SaaS Platform" (high multiple).
