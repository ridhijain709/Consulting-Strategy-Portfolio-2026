## 2. BUSINESS AUDIT TEMPLATE (For E-Commerce Founders)
**Product Name:** The E-Commerce Margin Audit
**Format:** Notion Template / Google Sheet

**Sections:**
1.  **CAC to LTV Ratio:** Are you acquiring customers organically, or are you dependent on performance marketing?
2.  **Product Margin Analysis:** What percentage of your sales are third-party (low margin) vs. private label (high margin)?
3.  **Return Rate Impact:** How do returns (especially in fashion/apparel) impact your reverse-logistics costs?
4.  **Brand Moat:** Do consumers trust your platform for authenticity, or do they just use you for discounts?

## 3. STRATEGY CHECKLIST (For MBA Interviews)
**Product Name:** The "Conglomerate Attack" Defense Checklist
*(Use this framework when asked how a startup beats Reliance/Tata/Amazon)*

*   [ ] **The Price War Veto:** Does the strategy rely on matching discounts? (If yes, it fails. Startups cannot outspend conglomerates).
*   [ ] **The Niche Exclusivity:** Does the strategy lock in exclusive supply that the conglomerate cannot access?
*   [ ] **The Community Moat:** Does the startup possess a highly engaged, organic community that acts as a structural CAC advantage?
*   [ ] **The Agility Premium:** Can the startup launch private labels or pivot supply chains faster than the bureaucracy of a conglomerate?
