# FREELANCING ASSETS: E-COMMERCE STRATEGY CONSULTING
**Objective:** Monetize analytical skills by offering Omnichannel & CAC Audits to D2C and E-Commerce Startups.

---

## 1. SAMPLE PROPOSAL TEMPLATE

**To:** [Client Name/CEO], [Company Name]
**From:** [Your Name], Independent Strategy Consultant
**Subject:** Proposal: Strategic Omnichannel & CAC Audit for [Company Name]

**1. Context & Objective**
[Company Name] has successfully scaled its digital presence, but rising performance marketing costs (Google/Meta) are eroding contribution margins. The objective of this engagement is to conduct a "Consulting-Grade" audit of your acquisition channels and product mix, modeled on the profitability playbooks of market leaders like Nykaa, to identify structural CAC advantages and high-margin product extensions.

**2. Scope of Work (The "Mini-MBB" Audit)**
Over a 3-week engagement, I will deliver a comprehensive strategic teardown focusing on:
*   **Phase 1: The CAC to LTV Audit:** Analyzing your dependency on paid acquisition vs. organic/content-driven acquisition.
*   **Phase 2: Margin Expansion Analysis:** Evaluating your current supplier mix and identifying opportunities for high-margin private label incubation.
*   **Phase 3: The Threat Matrix:** PESTLE & Porter's Five Forces analysis of your direct competitors and deep-pocketed conglomerates.

**3. Deliverables**
*   A 20-30 page Strategy Master Document (PDF).
*   A 15-slide Executive Board Presentation (PPT) tailored for investor pitches.
*   A 1-hour Strategy Read-Out Call with the founding team.

---

## 2. CLIENT DISCOVERY QUESTIONNAIRE

*Send this to the founder via Typeform/Google Forms before the first call.*

1.  What percentage of your sales come from organic search/content vs. paid performance marketing?
2.  What is the gross margin difference between your lowest-performing third-party product and your best-performing owned product?
3.  If Amazon or Reliance decides to discount your primary category by 30% tomorrow, why do your customers stay with you?
4.  What is the objective of your next 12 months? (e.g., Reach EBITDA positive, Double GMV, Expand Offline).

---

## 3. DISCOVERY CALL QUESTIONS (The "Consultant" Persona)

1.  "I see you are highly dependent on Meta ads. If the cost of an impression doubles next year, does your unit economic model break?"
2.  "Nykaa proved that holding inventory solves the 'authenticity' problem, but it drains working capital. How are you balancing inventory risk with customer experience?"
3.  "Are we treating physical retail as a sales channel, or as a customer acquisition billboard?"
