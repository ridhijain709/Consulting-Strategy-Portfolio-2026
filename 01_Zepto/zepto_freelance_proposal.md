# FREELANCING ASSETS: STRATEGY CONSULTING FOR STARTUPS
**Objective:** Monetize analytical skills by offering "Mini-MBB" Strategy Audits to Series A/B startups.

---

## 1. SAMPLE PROPOSAL TEMPLATE

**To:** [Client Name/CEO], [Company Name]
**From:** [Your Name], Independent Strategy Consultant
**Subject:** Proposal: Strategic Growth & Unit Economics Audit for [Company Name]

**1. Context & Objective**
[Company Name] has successfully achieved Product-Market Fit and is experiencing rapid top-line growth. However, scaling customer acquisition while defending contribution margins requires a rigid analysis of your underlying unit economics. The objective of this engagement is to conduct a "Consulting-Grade" audit of your business model to identify high-margin growth levers prior to your next funding round.

**2. Scope of Work (The "Mini-MBB" Audit)**
Over a 3-week engagement, I will deliver a comprehensive strategic teardown focusing on:
*   **Phase 1: Unit Economics Waterfall:** Analyzing AOV vs. CAC, fulfillment costs, and gross margins per transaction.
*   **Phase 2: Moat Evaluation (VRIO):** Identifying which of your capabilities are truly defensible against larger incumbents.
*   **Phase 3: Revenue Quality Analysis:** Mapping current revenue streams and identifying adjacent high-margin opportunities (e.g., retail media, subscriptions).
*   **Phase 4: Competitive Benchmarking:** PESTLE & Porter's Five Forces analysis of your direct competitors.

**3. Deliverables**
*   A 20-30 page Strategy Master Document (PDF).
*   A 15-slide Executive Board Presentation (PPT) tailored for investor pitches.
*   A 1-hour Strategy Read-Out Call with the founding team.

## 4. CLIENT DISCOVERY QUESTIONNAIRE

*Send this to the founder via Typeform/Google Forms before the first call.*

1.  What is the single biggest bottleneck to your growth right now? (Capital, Talent, CAC, Operations?)
2.  What is your exact Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) and how has it trended over the last 6 months?
3.  What is your Average Order Value (AOV) and gross margin per transaction?
4.  Who are your top 3 competitors, and why do customers choose you over them?
5.  What is the objective of your next 12 months? (e.g., Reach EBITDA positive, Double GMV, Raise Series B).

---

## 5. DISCOVERY CALL QUESTIONS (The "Consultant" Persona)
*(Ask these live on Zoom to establish absolute authority)*

1.  "I see your revenue is growing at X%, but your CAC is also rising. Where do you see the ceiling for your acquisition costs before unit economics break?"
2.  "If a massive incumbent (like Reliance/Amazon) enters this specific vertical tomorrow with unlimited capital, what is your structural defense?"
3.  "Are we treating all revenue equally right now, or have you segmented out your highest-margin product lines?"
4.  "What does your board want to see in the next 12 months that they aren't seeing today?"
