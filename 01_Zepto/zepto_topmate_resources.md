## 2. BUSINESS AUDIT TEMPLATE (For Founders)
**Product Name:** The Post-PMF Startup Audit Framework
**Format:** Notion Template / Google Sheet

**Sections:**
1.  **Unit Economics Check:**
    *   What is your true CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)?
    *   What is the LTV (Life Time Value) over 12 months?
    *   At what AOV does an individual transaction become profitable?
2.  **Moat Evaluation:**
    *   Are you competing on price/speed (temporary), or network effects/data (sustainable)?
3.  **Revenue Quality:**
    *   What percentage of your revenue is high-margin (e.g., software/ads) vs. low-margin (e.g., physical goods)?
4.  **Regulatory Blindspots:**
    *   Are you reliant on contractor loopholes? Data privacy grey areas?

## 3. STRATEGY CHECKLIST (For MBA Case Competitions)
**Product Name:** The Ultimate Case Competition Checklist
**Format:** 1-Page PDF

**Checklist Items:**
*   [ ] **The MECE Test:** Are all my solutions Mutually Exclusive and Collectively Exhaustive?
*   [ ] **The "So What?" Test:** Does every slide have an action title that states the conclusion, not just the data?
*   [ ] **The Unit Economics Reality Check:** Does my brilliant marketing idea ruin the profit margin per order?
