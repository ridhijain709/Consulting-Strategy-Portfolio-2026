**Title:** The $7 Billion Knapsack Problem: Why Zepto's Moat Isn't 10-Minute Delivery

*(Excerpt/Structure)*

**Section 1: The Myth of Speed**
10-minute delivery is a feature, not a moat. Flipkart and Amazon have the balance sheets to subsidize 10-minute delivery tomorrow if they wanted to. So why is Zepto valued at $7B? It comes down to VRIO (Valuable, Rare, Inimitable, Organized).

Explain the classic computer science "Knapsack Problem." Zepto treats its 2,500 sq ft dark stores not as supermarkets, but as dynamic knapsacks. Their AI dictates exactly which 4,000 SKUs go into which pin code based on hyper-local predictive demand (weather, time, local events).

**Conclusion:**
Zepto isn't competing with Kiranas. It's competing for the highest-margin digital shelf space in urban India.
