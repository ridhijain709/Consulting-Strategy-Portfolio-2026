# BLINKIT: LINKEDIN ENGINE & CAROUSEL

## 1. LONG ARTICLE (The CAC Moat)
**Title:** The Zomato Bailout that Created a $14B Juggernaut
**Excerpt:** When Zomato acquired Blinkit (Grofers) in 2022, critics called it a desperate bailout. Today, Blinkit is the crown jewel of the Zomato empire. Why? Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC). Zepto and Swiggy have to buy users on performance marketing channels. Blinkit simply taps into Zomato's millions of hungry users for free. This structural cost advantage is the only reason Blinkit can afford to command 45% of the market while pushing toward profitability.

## 2. PREMIUM POSTS
*   **Post 1 (Unit Economics):** Delivering an iPhone in 10 minutes isn't a gimmick; it's a survival strategy. At ₹400 AOV (Groceries), Blinkit loses money. At ₹70,000 AOV (iPhone), the fixed ₹40 delivery cost vanishes as a percentage of the margin. This is how Q-Commerce becomes profitable.
*   **Post 2 (Strategy):** Blinkit vs. Zepto. Zepto wins on algorithmic density. Blinkit wins on Top-of-Funnel ecosystem integration. Who wins the decade? 
*   **Post 3 (Contrarian):** Quick commerce isn't killing the Kirana store. It's killing Amazon. By stocking electronics, Blinkit is teaching consumers that 2-day delivery is too slow.
