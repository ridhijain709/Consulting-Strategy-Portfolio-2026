# BLINKIT: PORTFOLIO & RECRUITING ASSETS

*   Engineered a strategic teardown of Blinkit’s transition from scheduled grocery to a Q-Commerce market leader (45% share).

## 2. STAR INTERVIEW STORY
**Situation:** While analyzing Zepto, I realized I couldn't understand the market without understanding the 800lb gorilla: Blinkit. I wanted to map how an acquisition (Zomato buying Grofers) completely changed the unit economics of a sector.
**Task:** Build a consulting-grade analysis of Blinkit's structural moats.
**Action:** I analyzed Zomato's quarterly earnings calls. I mapped the CAC disparity. Zepto buys users; Blinkit cross-sells them for free. I also analyzed their AOV expansion strategy—delivering PlayStations and iPhones.
**Result:** I concluded that Blinkit is no longer a grocery app; it is "Amazon-in-10-minutes." This insight forms the core of my MBB interview portfolio, demonstrating I understand ecosystem leverage, not just standalone P&Ls.
